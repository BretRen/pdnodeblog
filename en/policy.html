Pdnode Microsoft Teams Policy

User Agreement and Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Microsoft Teams service provided by Pdnode. To ensure smooth and safe usage of Microsoft Teams by Pdnode team members, all users must comply with the following terms of use. This agreement applies to all employees, partners, and authorized users within Pdnode who use Microsoft Teams.

2. Purpose of Use

The Microsoft Teams service provided is mainly used for internal communication and collaboration within Pdnode, including but not limited to:

  • Daily work communication
  • Project management
  • Meetings and discussions
  • File sharing and collaboration

3. User Responsibilities

3.1 Legal Compliance

Users must comply with applicable laws and regulations when using Microsoft Teams, including but not limited to privacy laws, intellectual property laws, etc. Any illegal activities are not permitted by the Pdnode team and will be subject to appropriate penalties.

3.2 Content Management

It is prohibited to post any content on Teams that violates Pdnode team policies, such as:

  • Malicious messages, harassment, or abuse
  • Content that infringes on others' privacy or copyrights
  • Irrelevant advertisements or promotional messages

3.3 Confidentiality and Data Security

The confidentiality of internal Pdnode team information and data is crucial. Users must take reasonable measures to ensure the confidentiality of sensitive data and Pdnode team confidential information while using Microsoft Teams.

4. Prohibited Actions

4.1 Abuse of Features

It is prohibited to abuse any of the features provided by Teams, such as:

  • Sending spam
  • Performing unauthorized screen sharing or recording
  • Frequent interruptions or posting irrelevant information

4.2 Malicious Actions

Any form of malicious attack or actions intended to disrupt the Teams platform (e.g., hacking, data tampering, etc.) is strictly prohibited. Any such behavior will result in the immediate termination of access and may incur legal consequences.

5. Special Rules and Exceptions

5.1 Advertisements and Promotions

With explicit authorization from Pdnode team administrators, certain users may post work-related advertisements or promotional content within Microsoft Teams. Unauthorized posting of advertisements will be considered a violation.

5.2 Group Chat and Meeting Management

Users must not arbitrarily change group chat names, delete meeting records, or modify scheduled meetings. All actions should comply with the internal policies of the Pdnode team.

7. Service Usage and Support

7.1 Normal Service Usage

The Pdnode team will provide necessary technical support and service assurance to all employees using Microsoft Teams. Users can contact the IT department for assistance when facing technical issues.

7.2 Service Downtime Maintenance

The Pdnode team reserves the right to conduct regular maintenance or emergency downtime for the Teams service, but will notify all users in advance whenever possible. Users should resume normal use once the service is restored.

8. Changes and Termination of Agreement

8.1 Agreement Changes

Pdnode reserves the right to modify the terms of this agreement at any time. Any changes will be notified to all employees via the Pdnode intranet or email. Continued use of Microsoft Teams after the update will be considered as acceptance of the revised agreement.

8.2 Termination of Agreement

The Pdnode team reserves the right to terminate a user's access to Microsoft Teams at any time, particularly in cases of serious violations of this agreement.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this agreement, please contact the IT support team of Pdnode:

  • Email: gretren@pdnode.com
